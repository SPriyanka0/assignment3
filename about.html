Masala Chai is popular across the Indian subcontinet, as is more than just tea or in comparison to a simple cup of coffee. Chai is an important treat for most in the morning, for a symbol of hospitality, a refreshing hot drink in the middle of day, or something to drink when spending long night talking with friends about the same embarrasing stories. Late Night Chai came to be after a travel from india in which there was no place for friends to come together and simply relax while enjoying one another's presence after a long day of work. This hole in the wall was founded for such moments.
